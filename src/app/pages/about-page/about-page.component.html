About CoVex

A Coronavirus Explorer


CoVex (Coronavirus Explorer) is a unique online network and systems medicine platform for data analysis that integrates virus-human interactions for SARS-CoV-2 and SARS-CoV-1. It implements different network-based approaches for the identification of new drug targets and new repurposable drugs.

For detailed information please visit our blog: https://www.exbio.de/exbio-vs-covid-part-1/

We are constantly updating and integrating new data as it becomes available.
If you are interested to make your virus-human interactome data available via our platform, please contact us.

General workflow of the platform

  1. Select the viral interaction dataset (SARS-CoV1-Pfefferle, SARS-CoV1-VirHostNet, SARS-CoV2-Gordon).
  2. Select proteins of interest as seed proteins.
  3. Perform network analysis (multi-Steiner, TrustRank, KeyPathwayMiner, closeness centrality).
  4. Platform usage: basic and advanced options
  5. Inspect the resulting subnetworks.
  6. Find drugs associated with the subnetwork (TrustRank, closeness centrality).
  7. Download your results.

CoVex also provides a quick analysis option to perform steps 3 to 5 (starting from the seed proteins) to return a list of drugs ranked by default using TrustRank.
